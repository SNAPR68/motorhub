02 — Market Opportunity

The $36B
Trust Deficit

The Indian used car market is $36 billion (2025) heading to $83 billion by 2031. Yet 70.83% is still controlled by unorganized local dealers. Online platforms are growing at 26.85% CAGR, but trust remains the #1 barrier. CardDekho, Cars24, and OLX have scratched the surface — but left massive, painful gaps.

05 — Competitive Moat

Not a Listing Platform.
A Trust Engine.

Data Network Effect
Every inspection, every VehiclePassport, every transaction enriches the database. More data → better AI pricing → more accurate TrueCost → stronger buyer trust → more transactions. Competitors cannot replicate proprietary data moats without years of transactions.
Supply-Side Lock-In (DealerOS)
When unorganized dealers use our free tools to manage inventory, price cars, and access financing — they list with us first. Controlling 70% of supply means we control price discovery for the entire market.
Lifetime Buyer Relationship
PostPurchase Hub means we stay in the buyer's life from day 1 of ownership to the day they resell. When they sell, they sell through us. When they buy next, they buy through us. One acquisition, perpetual revenue.
Regulatory Integration
InstantRC + VehiclePassport require deep integrations with Vahan/Parivahan, OEM service portals, insurance aggregators, and RTO agent networks. This integration layer takes 12–18 months to build and cannot be fast-followed.
"CardDekho is an information portal. Cars24 is a transaction platform. OLX is a classifieds board. None of them are a Trust Engine. That's the gap. That's the opportunity."
